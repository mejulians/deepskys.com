Star in Pegasus
HIP 107315
Mag 2.40
Epsilon Pegasi, 8 Peg

  30/08/16
Really beautiful, used just to align finder and telescope but in 25mm so bright and so yellow, in real contrast to the 'arrow' asterism of much fainter 8th and 9th magnitude white stars
As I was observing a meteor flashed through the 25mm FOV, is this the first time I have observed a meteor through the eyepiece, amazing!
