25/03/17
Another great Galaxy in Canes Venatici, and this one is really unusual
At first glance it appears to be a bright Globular Cluster in 24mm, but zooming in does not resolve any stars, which is not surprising!
A very bright tight centre with a faint but distinct halo around it, not very big but unmistakable
Forms a nice triangle when zoomed in with HIP 62700 at Mag 9.60 below and a star of Mag 11.10 to it's left, very nice!
