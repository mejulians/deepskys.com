NGC 5907

Spiral Galaxy in Draco
NGC 5907 and NGC5906
Mag 10.4
  The Knife Edge or Splinter Galaxy

22/08/16
The very faintest sliver of this Mag 10.4 Galaxy in 12mm but easy to locate just below a neat asterism of stars of Mags 7.5, 7.9 and 8.3 with HIP 74458 in the middle at Mag 7.5 and HIP 74377 to the north at Mag 7.9
