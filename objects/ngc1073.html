Certainly easy to locate it's exact position within a spearhead shaped asterism in 12mm with a star of Mag 9.2 at it's point and an equilateral triangle of stars of Mags 9.7, 10.5 and 10.9 at it's base, NGC 1073 sitting nearly midway up thespearhead
