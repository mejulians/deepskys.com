Probably not the best evening to be observing double stars, what with the telescope shaking in the wind and clouds coming and going!
However, when things were calm and clear a great view using 12mm and Barlow to give 180x magnification which easily split each of the bright stars into its two components, although no feeling of differences in colour between the four stars
