IC 348


Star forming region and Open Cluster in Perseus
IC 348
 Mag 7.3

20/01/17
A very sparse and small cluster of 9th Mag and below with the notable exception of Atik, o Per, 38 Per, Hip17448 at Mag 3.85 although I assume Atik is not part of the cluster even though it appears to have nebulocity around it which is very bright. The rest of the cluster has the faintest hint of sitting in nebulocity
There are seven or so stars readily visible, of which the double (or treble!) star HIP17465 at Mag 9.25 is the brightest. It's B component at Mag 9.50 is not separable, but HIP17465C at Mag 10.25 is widely separated
There should be an unrelated Mag 12.15 star between them according to Stellarium but I can't see it
