This is a faint but resolved and really beautiful Open Cluster just outside the 12mm FOV from NGC 6946
Sits in a nice kite shaped asterism with HIP 101307 at Mag 7.15 at its base and stars of Mags 9.9, 10.0 and 10.45 at its head
Fantastic FOV
