This Galaxy sits in a very sparse FOV in both 12mm and 25 mm and the stars within it are faint and pattern less
However there is just a hint of haze in 12mm when I slowly move the FOV
I suspect that on a night of good seeing NGC 1637 would appear quite bright
However tonight it is low down through the atmosphere which is a bit poor
