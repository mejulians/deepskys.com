Binoculars

Details of the Binoculars I use for observing

Tento 7x35, a great value pair of binoculars made in the USSR, complete with a heavy duty leather case. Read a review here
Dated 1986, they were bought from Gifford Boyd in Robertson Street, Hastings

Oregon 20x80, with a FOV of 3.2° and a 4mm exit pupil giving 17mm eye relief
Made by Opticron, these binoculars were purchased following a very positive review from Ade Ashford in Astronomy Now in October 2019
For a while these binoculars have been my main observing instrument
The next project is to construct a mount for them
